## PART 3: OUTBOUND - Mondsee to Milan (Option B)

### **DAY 5: SUNDAY JUNE 29 - Mondsee to Cortina d'Ampezzo**
**Theme:** Into the Heart of the Dolomites  
**Driving:** ~250 km, 4.5 hours (with many scenic stops)

#### Late Morning: Depart Mondsee (11:00 AM)
- Sleep in after wedding
- Brunch at hotel
- Leisurely checkout

#### Midday: Through Austria to Italy (12:00 PM - 2:00 PM)

**Route:** Mondsee → Salzburg → Bischofshofen → Zell am See → Mittersill

**Optional Quick Salzburg Stop (1 hour):**
- Just Mirabell Gardens and Getreidegasse if desired
- Or drive straight through

**Scenic Drive:**
- **Hohe Tauern mountains**
- **Zell am See** (glimpse of lake)
- **Grossglockner option:** If you want to add the famous pass (2-3 hours extra)

#### Afternoon: Felbertauern Tunnel to Lienz (2:00 PM - 4:00 PM)

**Route:** Mittersill → Felbertauern Tunnel → Lienz

**Felbertauern Tunnel**
- 5.3 km tunnel through mountains
- Connects Salzburg region to East Tyrol
- Dramatic exit into different landscape

**Lienz (Optional Coffee Stop)**
- East Tyrol's main town
- Medieval Schloss Bruck
- **Coffee:** Café Zentral

#### Late Afternoon: Into the Dolomites (4:00 PM - 6:30 PM)

**Route:** Lienz → Sillian → San Candido → Cortina

**This is where it gets spectacular:**

**Sillian → San Candido (Border Crossing)**
- Cross from Austria to Italy
- Puster Valley scenery
- **San Candido:** Pretty alpine town (quick stop if time)

**Great Dolomite Road Approaches**
- Mountains become dramatic limestone peaks
- The famous Dolomite scenery unfolds

**Arrive Cortina d'Ampezzo (6:30 PM)**

**Cortina - "Pearl of the Dolomites"**
- Glamorous ski resort town
- Surrounded by iconic peaks
- Italian chic meets Alpine scenery

**Evening Exploration:**
- **Corso Italia:** Main pedestrian street
- **Piazza Roma:** Central square
- **Window shopping:** Luxury boutiques
- **Mountain views:** Every direction you look

**Dinner (8:00 PM)**
- **Restaurant Recommendation:** Tivoli
  - Elegant, traditional Cortina dining
  - Try: Canederli (bread dumplings), venison, polenta
  - Good wine list
  - Price: €40-60 per person

**Alternative:** El Camineto (more rustic, Ladin specialties)

**Evening:**
- **Aperitivo:** Bar Corso or Enoteca Cortina
  - Aperol Spritz with mountain views
  - Snacks included

### **DAY 6: MONDAY JUNE 30 - Cortina to Venice**
**Theme:** Dolomites Morning Spectacle & Grand Canal Arrival  
**Driving:** ~160 km, 2 hours (but spend morning in mountains)

#### Morning: Dolomites Exploration (9:00 AM - 1:00 PM)

**This is the highlight - take your time!**

**Option 1: Great Dolomite Road Loop (Recommended)**
- Cortina → Lake Misurina → Tre Cime → Passo Falzarego → Cortina
- **Time:** 3-4 hours with stops
- **Distance:** 100 km loop

**Lake Misurina (9:30 AM - 10:30 AM)**
- "Pearl of the Dolomites"
- Crystal clear lake reflecting Tre Cime
- **Coffee:** Lakeside café
- **Walk:** 30-minute lakeside stroll

**Tre Cime di Lavaredo Viewpoint (11:00 AM - 12:00 PM)**
- **The Three Peaks** - most iconic Dolomite view
- Drive to Rifugio Auronzo (€30 toll)
- 30-minute walk to famous viewpoint
- **Alternative:** View from below if not paying toll

**Passo Falzarego (12:30 PM - 1:00 PM)**
- 2,105m elevation
- WWI open-air museum (trenches, tunnels)
- **Lunch:** Mountain hut restaurant
  - Try: Kaiserschmarrn (shredded pancake), Speckknödel
  - Price: €15-25 per person

#### Afternoon: Drive to Venice (2:00 PM - 4:00 PM)

**Route:** Cortina → SS51 → A27 → Venice

**The Drive:**
- Descend from mountains to Veneto plains
- Dolomites fade into rolling hills
- Enter Venice lagoon area

**Arrive Venice (4:00 PM)**

**Important:** Return rental car at **Piazzale Roma**
- Venice is car-free
- Park in garage (€30-40 per day if staying overnight)
- Or return car and use vaporetto/walking

#### Late Afternoon/Evening: Venice Magic (5:00 PM - Evening)

**Vaporetto to San Marco** (if car returned)
- Water bus from Piazzale Roma
- See Grand Canal for first time

**St. Mark's Square (5:30 PM)**
- **St. Mark's Basilica:** Byzantine glory (free entry)
- **Doge's Palace:** Exterior marvel
- **Campanile:** Bell tower (€10 for elevator to top)
- **Caffè Florian:** Historic café (expensive but iconic)

**Rialto Bridge (7:00 PM)**
- Walk across iconic bridge
- **Rialto Market area:** Food stalls, bacari
- Views of Grand Canal

**Dinner (8:00 PM)**
- **Restaurant Recommendation:** Cantina Do Spade
  - Authentic bacaro (wine bar)
  - Cicchetti (Venetian tapas): Baccalà, sarde in saor, polpette
  - Stand at bar like locals
  - Price: €20-30 per person

**Alternative:** Osteria alle Testiere (seafood, book ahead)

**Evening:**
- **Get lost on purpose:** Wander backstreets
- **Canal views:** Every bridge is a photo opportunity
- **Night atmosphere:** Venice is quieter, more magical at night

**Overnight in Venice:**
- **Option 1:** Stay in Venice proper (expensive, magical)
- **Option 2:** Stay in Mestre (mainland, cheaper, easy train next day)

### **DAY 7: TUESDAY JULY 1 - Venice to Milan**
**Theme:** Morning in La Serenissima, Return Home  
**Driving:** 280 km, 3 hours (but no rush)

#### Morning: More Venice (9:00 AM - 12:00 PM)

**Relaxed Morning Options:**

**Option 1: Grand Canal by Vaporetto**
- Take Line 1 vaporetto from San Marco to Piazzale Roma
- 45-minute scenic journey
- See palaces from the water
- Like a cheap gondola tour

**Option 2: Dorsoduro Neighborhood**
- **Accademia Bridge:** Views of Grand Canal
- **Campo Santa Margherita:** Local square, students
- **Peggy Guggenheim Collection:** Modern art (if interested)
- **Zattere promenade:** Views to Giudecca island

**Option 3: Islands Tour (if staying extra day)**
- **Murano:** Glass blowing (45 min by vaporetto)
- **Burano:** Colorful houses, lace-making
- Would need to extend Venice stay

**Brunch (11:00 AM)**
- **Café:** Any bacaro near your location
  - Coffee, cicchetti
  - Or proper brunch at hotel

#### Late Morning: Depart Venice (12:00 PM)

**If Stayed in Mestre:**
- Already on mainland
- Drive straight to Milan

**If Stayed in Venice:**
- Vaporetto to Piazzale Roma
- Pick up car (if kept overnight)
- Or take train (see below)

#### Afternoon: Return to Milan (12:00 PM - 3:00 PM)

**Driving:** Venice → A4 Autobahn → Milan
- Straightforward 2.5-3 hour drive
- **Stop options:**
  - **Padua (Padova):** Historic university town (30 min detour)
  - **Vicenza:** Palladian architecture (1 hour detour)
  - **Verona:** If you skipped it (1 hour detour)

**Train Alternative:**
- Leave car in Venice (Mestre)
- Take **high-speed train Venice → Milan** (2.5 hours)
- Frecciarossa or Italo
- From €20-50 per person
- Arrive Milan Centrale

**Arrive Milan (3:00 PM)**
- Return rental car
- Trip complete!
