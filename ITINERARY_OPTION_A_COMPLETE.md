# ITINERARY OPTION A: Lake Como, Innsbruck, South Tyrol & Verona
## Milan → Mondsee → Bolzano → Verona → Milan

**Dates:** June 24-30, 2026 (7 days)  
**Theme:** Alpine Lakes, Mountain Passes, Wine Country & Romance  
**Pace:** Relaxed with time to enjoy scenery and meals

---

## PART 1: INBOUND - Milan to Mondsee

### **DAY 1: TUESDAY JUNE 24 - Milan to Chiavenna**
**Theme:** Lake Como Beauty & Alpine Approach  
**Driving:** ~140 km, 2.5 hours (with stops)

#### Morning: Depart Milan (9:00 AM)
- Pick up rental car in Milan
- Quick coffee near rental location
- Head north on A9 toward Lake Como

#### Late Morning: Lake Como Exploration (10:30 AM - 2:00 PM)

**Varenna (10:30 AM - 12:30 PM)**
- **Walk of Lovers (Passeggiata degli Innamorati):** 30-minute scenic lakeside stroll
- **Villa Monastero:** Beautiful gardens extending to the lake (€10 entry, allow 45 min)
- **Varenna Old Town:** Narrow cobblestone streets, colorful houses
- **Photo stops:** Classic Lake Como views with Alps backdrop

**Lunch in Varenna (12:30 PM - 2:00 PM)**
- **Restaurant Recommendation:** Il Cavatappi
  - Lakeside terrace, fresh lake fish (lavarello)
  - Try: Risotto with perch, local wine
  - Price: €25-35 per person
  - Reserve outdoor table

#### Afternoon: Scenic Drive to Chiavenna (2:00 PM - 5:00 PM)

**Route:** Varenna → Bellagio (ferry or drive around) → Menaggio → Colico → Chiavenna

**Bellagio (Optional - 1 hour)**
- **The Pearl of Lake Como**
- Walk the stepped streets (Salita Serbelloni)
- Gelato at Gelateria del Borgo
- Skip if running behind - Chiavenna is the priority

**Menaggio to Colico (Scenic Lakeside Drive)**
- Follow SP72 along Lake Como's northern shore
- Stop at **Piona Abbey** (12th century, free entry, 20 min)
- Views of Grigne mountains across the lake

#### Evening: Arrive Chiavenna (5:00 PM)

**Chiavenna - Your Italian Alpine Base**
- Historic town at the foot of Val Bregaglia
- Medieval center with porticoed streets
- Gateway to Maloja Pass

**Late Afternoon Exploration:**
- **Historic Center:** Piazza Salis, Collegiata di San Lorenzo
- **Bregaglia Valley Viewpoint:** Walk to overlook the valley entrance
- **Relaxing stroll:** Via Prénugola (main street with shops)

**Dinner (8:00 PM)**
- **Restaurant Recommendation:** Osteria del Porto
  - Local Valtellina cuisine
  - Try: Pizzoccheri (buckwheat pasta), Bresaola
  - Good wine list from nearby Valtellina valley
  - Price: €30-40 per person
  - Outdoor seating available

**Alternative:** Locanda della Torre Antica (historic building, cozy atmosphere)

**Evening:** Walk along the Mera River or enjoy a digestif at a café in Piazza

---

### **DAY 2: WEDNESDAY JUNE 25 - Chiavenna to Innsbruck**
**Theme:** Spectacular Alpine Passes  
**Driving:** ~280 km, 4-5 hours (with stops)

#### Morning: Depart Chiavenna (9:00 AM)
- Breakfast at hotel or **Caffè delle Alpi** (good coffee, pastries)
- Fill up with fuel (last Italian gas = cheaper)
- Buy Swiss vignette if entering Switzerland briefly

#### Late Morning: The Maloja Pass (10:00 AM - 12:00 PM)

**Chiavenna → Maloja Pass (45 min)**
- **Val Bregaglia Scenic Drive:** Narrow valley with granite peaks
- **Soglio:** (Optional detour - 20 min) Gorgeous mountain village with stone houses
- **Castasegna:** Border village, last stop in Switzerland if taking shortcut

**Maloja Pass Summit (1,815m)**
- **Stop at:** Maloja Palace area for photos
- **Views:** Engadine Valley opening up
- **Lake Sils (Silsersee):** Briefly visible from the pass
- **Time:** 20-30 minutes for photos, coffee at pass hotel

**Coffee at Maloja:** Hotel Maloja Kulm (terrace with panoramic views)

#### Midday: Through Engadine to Reschen Pass (12:00 PM - 2:00 PM)

**Route:** Maloja → St. Moritz area → Ofen Pass OR Reschen Pass → Nauders

**Option 1: Ofen Pass (Fuorn Pass)**
- More scenic, through Swiss National Park
- Higher elevation (2,149m)
- Wilder landscape

**Option 2: Reschen Pass (Recommended)**
- **Reschensee with submerged church tower** (iconic photo spot)
- Easier driving
- Passo di Resia (1,504m)
- **Stop for 30 min:** Walk to the bell tower in the lake

**Lunch at Reschen Pass Area (1:00 PM)**
- **Restaurant:** Reschener Stube or Seegasthof (near the submerged church)
  - South Tyrolean specialties
  - Try: Schlutzkrapfen (spinach ravioli), Speckknödel
  - Views of the lake and bell tower
  - Price: €20-30 per person

#### Afternoon: Into Austria - Landeck to Innsbruck (3:00 PM - 6:00 PM)

**Route:** Nauders → Landeck → Innsbruck
- Descend into Tyrol via Inn Valley
- **Arlberg Pass** alternative if you want more mountain driving
- Or take the faster A12 Autobahn through the valley

**Optional Stop - Wattens (30 min detour):**
- **Swarovski Crystal Worlds** (Kristallwelten)
- Giant's garden with crystal installations
- Entry: €19, allow 1 hour if interested

#### Evening: Arrive Innsbruck (5:30-6:00 PM)

**Innsbruck - Capital of the Alps**
- Surrounded by 2,000m+ peaks
- Imperial history meets mountain culture

**Evening Exploration (6:30 PM):**
- **Old Town Stroll:** Herzog-Friedrich-Straße
- **Golden Roof (Goldenes Dachl):** Iconic landmark, 2,657 fire-gilded copper tiles
- **Imperial Palace (Hofburg):** Exterior view, baroque architecture
- **Maria-Theresien-Straße:** Main pedestrian street, mountain backdrop

**Dinner (8:00 PM)**
- **Restaurant Recommendation:** Stiftskeller
  - Historic cellar restaurant
  - Traditional Tyrolean: Gröstl (pan-fried potatoes with meat), Kasspatzen
  - Good beer selection
  - Price: €25-35 per person

**Alternative:** Ottoburg (medieval tower, elegant atmosphere, slightly pricier)

**Evening Activity:**
- **Nordkette by Night:** Take funicular up for city lights view (runs until 11 PM)
- Or stroll along the Inn River

### **DAY 3: THURSDAY JUNE 26 - Innsbruck to Mondsee**
**Theme:** Morning in Innsbruck, Easy Drive to Wedding  
**Driving:** ~180 km, 2.5 hours

#### Morning: Explore Innsbruck (9:00 AM - 12:30 PM)

**Relaxed Morning with Time to Enjoy:**

**Option 1: Nordkette Mountain Experience (Recommended)**
- **Hungerburgbahn Funicular** from city center
- Seegrube (1,905m) - panoramic terrace
- **Time:** 2-3 hours round trip
- **Cost:** ~€40 return
- **Views:** Inn Valley, Karwendel mountains

**Option 2: Old Town Deep Dive**
- **Hofkirche (Court Church):** 28 bronze statues (including Maximilian I's cenotaph)
- **Cathedral of St. James:** Baroque masterpiece
- **Market Hall (Markthalle):** Local produce, coffee
- **Time:** 2 hours

**Option 3: Ambras Castle (Schloss Ambras)**
- Renaissance castle with art collections
- Spanish Hall is spectacular
- 20 min drive from center
- **Time:** 2-3 hours

**Brunch/Lunch (12:00 PM)**
- **Café Recommendation:** Café Mundingplatz or Cafe Kröll
  - Tyrolean breakfast items
  - Good coffee
  - Outdoor seating
  - Price: €15-20 per person

#### Afternoon: Drive to Mondsee (1:30 PM - 4:00 PM)

**Route:** Innsbruck → Wörgl → St. Johann in Tirol → Lofer → Salzburg → Mondsee

**Scenic Option:** Via Kufstein (fortress town, 30 min from Innsbruck)
- **Kufstein Fortress:** Largest fortress in Tyrol, organ concerts
- Walk around old town (1 hour max)

**Drive Through:**
- **Wilder Kaiser mountains** (dramatic limestone peaks)
- **Loferer Alm:** Alpine plateau views
- **Salzburg outskirts:** Brief glimpse of fortress

**Arrive Mondsee by 4:00 PM** (2 hours before wedding - perfect!)

#### Evening: Pre-Wedding in Mondsee
- Check into accommodation
- **Relaxing stroll:** Mondsee lakeside promenade
- **Visit:** Mondsee Abbey (Basilika) - Sound of Music wedding church
- **Drink:** Coffee/cake at lakeside café
- **Photos:** Drachenwand (dragon wall) cliffs across the lake
- **Dinner:** Light meal at hotel or nearby restaurant

## PART 3: OUTBOUND - Mondsee to Milan (Option A)

### **DAY 5: SUNDAY JUNE 29 - Mondsee to Bolzano**
**Theme:** Salzburg Stop & South Tyrol Entry  
**Driving:** ~280 km, 4 hours (with stops)

#### Late Morning: Depart Mondsee (11:00 AM)
- Sleep in after wedding
- Brunch at hotel
- Leisurely checkout

#### Midday: Salzburg Stop (12:00 PM - 3:00 PM)

**Quick Salzburg Highlights (3 hours):**

**Mirabell Gardens (30 min)**
- Free entry
- Sound of Music "Do-Re-Mi" steps
- Baroque gardens, fountain

**Getreidegasse (1 hour)**
- Mozart's Birthplace (yellow house)
- Historic shopping street
- Famous wrought-iron signs
- **Lunch here:** Café Tomaselli (historic coffee house, Mozart's favorite)

**Hohensalzburg Fortress (Optional - 1.5 hours)**
- Take funicular up
- Views over Salzburg
- Skip if short on time

**Lunch in Salzburg (1:00 PM)**
- **Restaurant Recommendation:** Café Tomaselli
  - Historic café since 1703
  - Coffee, cake, light lunch
  - Traditional atmosphere
  - Price: €15-25 per person

**Alternative:** Augustiner Bräustübl (beer hall, massive beer garden)

#### Afternoon: Drive to Bolzano (3:00 PM - 6:00 PM)

**Route:** Salzburg → Bischofshofen → Pass Gschütt → Golling → Werfen → Radstadt → Altenmarkt → Flachau → Ennstal → Liezen → Admont → Hieflau → Leoben → Bruck an der Mur → Graz → Klagenfurt → Villach → Tarvisio → Udine → Tolmezzo → Ampezzo → **BUT THIS IS TOO LONG**

**Better Route:** Salzburg → Hallein → Werfen → Pfarrwerfen → Golling → Kuchl → Hallein → Anif → Salzburg → A10 → **Actually, simpler:**

**Actual Best Route:** Salzburg → Bischofshofen → Zell am See → Mittersill → Felbertauern Tunnel → Lienz → Bruneck → Brixen → Bolzano

**OR easier:** Salzburg → A10 Autobahn → Villach → Udine → A23 → Bolzano

**Via A10 (Easier, Faster):**
- Salzburg → Tauern Autobahn (A10)
- Villach
- Udine (Italy)
- Tarvisio
- Autobahn A23
- Bolzano

**Scenic Stops Along the Way:**
- **Werfen:** Hohenwerfen Fortress (visible from highway)
- **Golling:** Waterfall just off highway (15 min detour)
- **Villach:** Coffee break, last town before Italy

#### Evening: Arrive Bolzano (6:00-7:00 PM)

**Bolzano (Bozen) - Where Austria Meets Italy**
- Bilingual German-Italian culture
- German efficiency + Italian charm
- Gateway to Dolomites

**Evening Exploration:**
- **Piazza Walther:** Central square, cathedral views
- **Lauben (Portici):** Arcaded shopping streets
- **Fruit Market:** Local produce, speck, cheese
- **Victory Monument:** Views over town

**Dinner (8:00 PM)**
- **Restaurant Recommendation:** Hopfen & Co.
  - Craft beer + South Tyrolean food
  - Modern atmosphere
  - Try: Knödel variations, local beers
  - Price: €25-35 per person

**Alternative:** Wirtshaus Atheiner (traditional, more rustic)

**Evening:** Walk along Talvera River or enjoy the mountain air

### **DAY 6: MONDAY JUNE 30 - Bolzano to Verona**
**Theme:** South Tyrol Morning & Romantic Verona  
**Driving:** ~150 km, 2 hours (with long stops)

#### Morning: Bolzano Exploration (9:00 AM - 1:00 PM)

**Relaxed Morning:**

**Ötzi the Iceman (Optional - 1.5 hours)**
- **South Tyrol Museum of Archaeology**
- 5,300-year-old mummy from glacier
- Incredibly well-preserved
- Entry: €10
- **Unique:** Worth seeing if interested in archaeology

**OR Just Wander:**
- **Old Town:** Medieval streets, market square
- **Cathedral:** Gothic-Romanesque mix
- **Café Culture:** Sit at Piazza Walther
- **Shopping:** Local woodwork, wool products, wine

**Brunch/Lunch (12:00 PM)**
- **Café Recommendation:** Caffè Macchiato or Cafe Buratti
  - Coffee, pastries, light lunch
  - People watching
  - Price: €15-20 per person

**Alternative:** Market stalls for street food

#### Afternoon: Drive to Verona (1:30 PM - 4:00 PM)

**Route:** Bolzano → A22 Autobahn → Trento → Rovereto → Verona

**Optional Stop - Trento (45 min):**
- **Cathedral Square:** Renaissance architecture
- **Buonconsiglio Castle:** Historic fortress
- **Moser Tower:** Views over the city
- Just off the highway

**Arrive Verona (4:00 PM)**

#### Late Afternoon/Evening: Verona Romance (4:30 PM - Evening)

**Verona - City of Romeo & Juliet**

**Piazza delle Erbe (5:00 PM)**
- Roman forum turned market square
- Whimsical fountain (Madonna Verona)
- Cafés with outdoor seating
- **Aperitivo time:** Order Aperol Spritz, get snacks

**Juliet's House (Casa di Giulietta)**
- Famous balcony (actually added in 1936!)
- Bronze statue of Juliet (rub breast for luck)
- Love letters on walls
- **Time:** 30 minutes
- **Entry:** Courtyard free, house €6

**Arena di Verona (6:30 PM)**
- Third-largest Roman amphitheater (after Rome, Capua)
- Incredibly well-preserved
- **Best view:** From Piazza Bra
- **Evening:** If opera is playing, consider tickets
- **Entry:** €10 to enter, €20-200 for opera

**Dinner (8:00 PM)**
- **Restaurant Recommendation:** Trattoria al Pompiere
  - Traditional Veronese cuisine
  - Try: Amarone risotto, horse meat (local specialty)
  - Excellent wine list (Valpolicella, Amarone)
  - Price: €35-50 per person
  - Reserve ahead

**Alternative:** Osteria al Duca (near Arena, casual, good value)

**Evening Stroll:**
- **Ponte Pietra:** Roman bridge over Adige River
- **Castelvecchio:** Medieval castle (exterior)
- **River views:** Sunset colors on the water

- **Grotte di Catullo:** Roman ruins

- **Lunch:** Lakeside restaurant

**Option 3: Valpolicella Wine Country**
- **Wine tasting:** Amarone country (30 min from Verona)
- **Villa della Torre:** Renaissance villa, winery
- **Lunch:** Winery restaurant with views
- Depart around 1:00 PM

#### Afternoon: Return to Milan (1:00 PM - 3:00 PM)

**Drive:** Verona → A4 Autobahn → Milan
- Straightforward highway drive
- **Stop:** Brescia or Bergamo (if time permits)

**Arrive Milan (3:00 PM)**
- Return rental car
- Trip complete!
